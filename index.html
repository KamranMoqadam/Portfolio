ABOUT ME

I am a seasoned software developer with extensive experience in Python programming and a broad skill set across various technology domains. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence. My academic journey also includes a master's degree in Information Technology and a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering. Over the years, I have gained valuable experience in both industrial and academic settings as a web designer, software developer, and programming mentor.

My expertise primarily revolves around Python, and I have extensive experience in web development using frameworks such as Django, Flask, and FastAPI. I am also highly skilled in database management, specializing in PostgreSQL, Redis, and MongoDB. On the front-end side, I have experience in crafting modern and visually appealing web applications using HTML, CSS, and the Tailwind CSS framework.

Beyond web development, I actively work in the field of Data Science, leveraging machine learning and deep learning techniques to solve complex problems. I specialize in processing and analyzing large datasets, working with time-series data, and implementing advanced predictive models. Additionally, I have deep expertise in image processing and object detection, utilizing tools like OpenCV, TensorFlow, and PyTorch to develop intelligent systems capable of recognizing and classifying objects with high accuracy.

Currently, I am working on pipeline inspection data analysis in the oil and gas industry, where I apply advanced machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques to detect pipeline defects, improve efficiency, and enhance predictive maintenance strategies.

My passion for technology extends beyond industrial applications. My undergraduate project was focused on developing a 3D animation to promote environmental protection, demonstrating my commitment to sustainability and ecological awareness. Furthermore, my master's thesis explored sentiment analysis in social networks using a hybrid approach combining Deep Belief Networks (DBN) and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) models, showcasing my expertise in natural language processing and deep learning.

I am deeply interested in environmental conservation and sustainability, believing that technology can play a crucial role in addressing global ecological challenges. Whether through AI-driven solutions for energy efficiency, smart environmental monitoring systems, or innovative visualization techniques, I am committed to leveraging my expertise to contribute to a more sustainable future.

My technical proficiency extends to automation and web scraping, where I employ Selenium for efficient data extraction and interaction with web content. I am also experienced in containerization with Docker, task queuing with Celery and RabbitMQ, and proficient in Linux-based system administration.

With a strong foundation in artificial intelligence, software development, and data science, I continuously seek to push the boundaries of technology, delivering innovative solutions that drive progress in both industry and environmental sustainability.
